# [Nonviolence Ontology](https://operarioribeiro.github.com/NonviolenceOntology)

> _"Nonviolent communication requires a controlled vocabulary."_

## Open Project:

**Nonviolence Ontology** is a [semantic web]([controlled vocabulary](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Controlled_vocabulary)) [ontology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ontology_(information_science)) [controlled vocabulary](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Controlled_vocabulary) for [nonviolent communication](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nonviolent_Communication) and [nonviolent tactics](https://www.tactics.nonviolenceinternational.net) that can be used for [artificial intelligence](https://www.aifreecert.com) [open ethics](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.18209465) [regulatory compliance](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Regulatory_compliance).

![image](https://github.com/operarioribeiro/NonviolenceOntology/blob/main/Nonviolence%20Ontology.png)


## Open Methodology:

1. [Rule of law (RoL)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rule_of_law) [regulatory compliance](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Regulatory_compliance) [secure operational development (DevSecOps)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DevOps#DevSecOps,_shifting_security_left).
2. [First do no harm](https://firstdonoharm.dev/) [ethical source](https://ethicalsource.dev/) [open licensing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open_license).
[contributor covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) [code of conduct](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Code_of_conduct).
3. [Open source semantic web ontology (OSSWO)](https://www.academia.edu/145734647/Open_Source_Semantic_Web_Ontology_OSSWO_).
4. [Artificial intelligence free certification (AI Free Cert)](https://www.aifreecert.com) [open science](https://www.protocols.io/view/rule-of-law-open-science-rolos-81wgbzom3gpk/v1).
5. [Compassionate listening](https://sosspeace.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/The-Five-Practices-of-Compassionate-ListeningSM-2.pdf) [open governance](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open-source_governance#Applications_of_the_principles) [e-voting](https://democracy.foundation/similar-projects/).
6. [Startup](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Startup_company) [open business](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open_business) [open architecture](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/TOGAF) [constitution deelopment](https://constitutionnet.org/).
7. [Nonviolent communication](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nonviolent_Communication) and [nonviolent tactics](https://www.tactics.nonviolenceinternational.net/) [controlled vocabulary](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Controlled_vocabulary) for the regulation of [educational resources](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open_educational_resources), [educational practices](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open_pedagogy), and [artificial intelligences (AIs)](https://www.ecosia.org/search?q=ai+regulation).
